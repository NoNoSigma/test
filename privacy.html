Privacy Policy for FusionTalk Messages

Effective Date: May 21, 2025

Welcome to FusionTalk Messages. Your privacy is incredibly important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data—spoiler: we don’t sell it, and we don’t want to spy on you either.

1. What We Collect

  • Email address or username (for account creation)
  • Messages you send and receive (stored securely via Firebase)
  • App usage info (basic, anonymous analytics for improvement)

2. How We Use Your Information

We only use your information to:

  • Operate and improve the app
  • Enable messaging functionality
  • Authenticate and manage your account

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your personal info with advertisers or third parties.
  • We do not access your messages unless required by law or for safety/security checks.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored using Firebase, a secure and encrypted service by Google. We use authentication, access controls, and encryption to protect your data. Only authorized system admins can access certain information when absolutely necessary.

5. Deleting Your Data

You can request account deletion by contacting us. We will permanently erase your data from our servers within 30 days.

6. Updates

We may update this policy as FusionTalk evolves. If we make significant changes, we’ll notify you directly via the app.
